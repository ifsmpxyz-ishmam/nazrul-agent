FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a C&F Agent? +
A Clearing and Forwarding (C&F) Agent is a licensed professional who handles customs clearance, documentation, and logistics on behalf of importers and exporters. We act as the bridge between you and customs authorities.
How long does customs clearance take? +
With complete documentation, most shipments are cleared within 24-48 hours. Complex shipments or those requiring additional inspection may take 3-5 working days. We always aim for the fastest turnaround possible.
What documents do I need for import clearance? +
Key documents include: Bill of Lading/Airway Bill, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Insurance Certificate, and Letter of Credit (if applicable). We guide you through every document required for your specific cargo type.
Do you operate at all ports in Bangladesh? +
Yes, we operate at Chittagong Port, Dhaka ICD, Mongla Port, Benapole Land Port, and all major air cargo terminals including Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport.
How do I get a quote? +
Simply fill out our contact form below or call us directly. Share your cargo details — type, weight, origin, destination — and we will provide a detailed quote within 2 hours.
